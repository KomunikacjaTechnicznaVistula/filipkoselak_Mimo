Elements

Other elements of Mimo app

  • Leaderboard

    On leaderboard you can see your current standings, check your league and see your competition. The leaderboard is updated every week. There are five leagues, starting from Wooden. You have to be in the top 10 to advance to the next league.

  • Community

    You can use this feature to communicate with the community of learners and look for useful information.

  • Mimo store

    You receive coins after completing lessons. You can access Mimo Store to exchange your coins for: streak freeze, streak repair and league protection. With streak freeze you can keep your streak going if you have 24 hours of inactivity. You can use streak repair to repair the most recent day of inactivity. With league protection you can protect yourself from dropping to a lower league when you end up in the danger zone.

  • Activity

    Activity tabs shows you the days when you completed lessons. It shows you your current streak as well. The streak is the number of days in a row you have completed a lesson. Your streak will increase by 1 day once you finish a lesson.

  • Playgrounds

    Playground is an option that allows you to practice what you have learned.

Using other functions of Mimo

How to access additional functions of Mimo (leaderboard, community, mimo store, activity calendar,playground)

  1. Leaderboard
    1. To access the leaderboard click on the "cup" icon below.
    2. Now you can see your league and your rank.
  2. Community
    1. Click on "Community" icon below to acces the forum.
    2. Now you can search for any topic on a community forum.
  3. Mimo Store
    1. Click on the "coin" icon to access Mimo Store.
    2. Now you can see the Mimo Store.
  4. Activity
    1. Click on the "flame" icon to acces Activity Calendar.
    2. Now you can see the Activity Calendar.
  5. Playground
    1. Click on the "profile" tab to access playgrounds.
    2. Click on "CREATE" button to create a playground.
    3. Select a template from: Python program, JavaScript program, Simple website and React app.
    4. You can code anything you want in the playground.
