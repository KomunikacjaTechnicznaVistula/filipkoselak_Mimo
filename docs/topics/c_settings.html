How to change the settings

Account setup, password reset, deleting an account, logging out

  1. Open settings.
    1. Click on "profile" icon below.
    2. Click on "settings" icon in the upper right corner.
    3. Now you can change the settings.
  2. Change profile photo.
    1. Click on "change profile picture".
    2. Select the source of your new photo.
    3. Select the photo.
    4. Adjust the photo.
    5. Now press on "crop" in the upper right corner.
  3. Set up the sound effects.
    1. Click on the toggle bar to switch it on or off.
  4. Change password.
    1. Click on "change password".
    2. Introduce your e-mail addres in "your email" window.
    3. Click on "reset password" button.
  5. Delete account.
    1. Click on "delete account".
    2. Click on "delete" button to complete the delete.
  6. Log out.
    1. Click on "log out".
